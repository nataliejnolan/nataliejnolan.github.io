Punishments

Punishments are the biggest part of every episode. At the end of each episode, whoever lost the most challenges
must complete a punishment given to them by the other guys. The rule is that you can never say no to a
punishment no matter what it is. The punishments range from physical challenges to embarrassing tasks to
psychological games. The total amount of punishments per jokers is:

  1. Joe→ 47 punishments
  2. Q→ 61 punishments
  3. Murr→ 76 punishments
  4. Sal→ 85 punishments

Some of the best punishments on the show are:

  • The Tattoo Punishment→ Joe won this episode, leaving Sal, Murr, and Q to all be punished. Joe had
    them all get tattoos without being able to see what they were until it was finished.
  • The Marriage Punishment→ Sal was being punished this episode and they had him strapped to a two-wheeler
    while Murr married his younger sister. In the church, they had all of their family and friends
    to watch the ceremony
  • The Genie Punishment→ Joe was being punished this episode and they had him be the Genie in a small play,
    without knowing his lines or what the play was about. They hooked him up to a harness and made
    him “fly” crashing into and destroying the set when least expected.
  • The Interview Punishment→ Murr was being punished this episode and they made him believe he was going to
    a weightlifting competition, so they oiled him up and sent him in. Only to find out he was
    walking into a room where he would have to interview his childhood celebrity crush, Danica McKeller.
  • The Tarantula Punishment→ Q was being punished this episode and one of his biggest fears is spiders. They
    took him to a warehouse and strapped him to a bed where they brought out tarantula spiders
    and had them crawl on him while strapped down. This punishment was hard to watch because you could see the
    pain he was in.
