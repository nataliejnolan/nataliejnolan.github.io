The Guys

Joseph "Joe" Gatto

Joe was born on June 5, 1976 in New York. He is argued to be the “funniest joker,” as he is the one to do almost
anything and everything. Joe left Impractical Jokers in 2022 and is currently doing stand-up tours along with
podcasting. Before Impractical Jokers, he worked as a baby store salesman.

Salvatore "Sal" Vulcano

Sal was born on November 5, 1976 in New York. He is known to be the joker who is the most scared and embarrassed,
which makes him a favorite. Along with the show, he is a popular stand-up comedian and is host to multiple
podcasts. Before the show, he owned and bartender a bar in New York.

Brian "Q" Quinn

Q was born on March 14, 1976 in New York. He does not get the appreciation he deserves, he also does almost anything
the guys dare him to do along with getting some of the worst punishments. He has started his own beer
company called Rubsam & Horrmann Brewing Company on Staten Island. Before the show Q was a NYC Firefighter.

James "Murr" Murray

Murr was born on May 1, 1976 in New York. He is known to be the most annoying joker, as the rest of them get frequently
annoyed with him on set. He is also known for having the worst and most disgusting punishments. Along
with the show he is also an author, and before the show he attended Georgetown University in Washington DC.

How They Met

All of the guys met as freshmen in high school, at an all boys catholic high school in Staten Island, New York. They
quickly bonded over their humor and became very close friends. In their twenties, they created a comedy
group called The Tenderloins, where they produced sketch comedy and entered contests. They eventually won a
contest with their sketch called The Craig Murray Incident, and won a cash prize which they put towards
creating a pilot episode for Impractical Jokers.
