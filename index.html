What is it?

Impractical Jokers is an American television show about four best friends, Joe Gatto, Sal Vulcano, Brian Quinn,
and James Murray, who dare each other to prank and perform embarrassing tasks in public. The show revolves
around hidden-camera scenarios where each member takes turns performing embarrassing tasks, as directed by
the other guys through an earpiece. If they refuse or fail to perform a task they lose, making it high stakes to do any
and everything asked. The show is known for the chemistry between the friends, the improvisation, as well as the
lighthearted humor of the pranks. Impractical Jokers is currently on their 11th season, with the first season premiering
in 2011 and having been an American comedy fan-favorite ever since.

Each task that they compete in is called a challenge. There are various challenges that they compete in throughout the seasons.
Some favorites are:
  1. The Try Not To Laugh Challenge → Where all four members are in a waiting room, doing crazy things to try
    and make each other laugh. Whoever is the last in the waiting room without laughing wins.
  2. Focus Group Challenges → Where one of the guys will be participating in a focus group doing whatever the
    other guys say, despite being in a room answering questions with multiple other people.
  3. Presentations → Occasionally, one or two of the guys will have to give a presentation to an audience
    without knowing anything about the presentation or even the topic of it. These can be the funniest episodes
    because it's a surprise to the guys as well as the public.
An episode typically consists of two or three challenges, having the member who loses the most tasks get punished.
A punishment happens at the end of every episode, and the cast member can not back down from whatever
it is since it is their punishment.
